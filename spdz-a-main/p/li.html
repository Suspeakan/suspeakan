Particle: Li

The particle ("партикол походкухамё", linguistic particle, not physical particle), ли, is a very common СЫПЕҖ word; this is because it is the more semantically important component of the middle voice construction. For an explaination of the middle voice (сорат касамонамё ду пулйлоцкацы), see Suspeakan, 2023-09-09.

In the middle voice construction, ли is placed before the verb, but in the reflexive construction, which is the middle with different ли placement, it is put before the subject.

// Ли йо̄ яйпам ок // MID(.RFLXV) 1sg love also

In the above example, the first person pronoun, йо̄, is after ли, meaning it, being the subject, is acting upon itself.

Ли was first created with the example of "ли рицы", meaning,"it rains" or "it's raining", and ли still retains the aspect it was given back at creation, and that is "pronominality". Like in the aforementioned Suspeakan, 2023-09-09, (paraphrasing and taking to logical conclusion) "natural processes, which have no agency, are not arguments like the agent and patient are; thus, neccesarily, a sentence containg one must be in the middle voice." Ли can serve as a pronoun to replace the said natural process—that is why it is not "цэн ли рицы (минтиш)" ((the) sky rains (here)), as ли assumes the natural "force" which neccesarily performs the natural process, it neccesarily rains from the sky for an example, et cetera.

Some more examples of this include:

  • ли пойжизопы¹
    (or ли пойжы)
    Fire/it burns
  • ли лакадоликомиры
    it (is) increases(/ing)
  • ли крсацихы
    (the) sun is shining (or, it's bright)

If attempting to translate, including the assumed natural force is still acceptable.


[1] ли may also assume "it"... a pronoun, of a pronoun in some situations...
