Syllable Structure: (C)(C)(C)(V)(C)(C)(C), in a cluster of three, one of them must be a plosive, and a plosive that would not form a affricate with another fricative, so no /tʂt͡ʂ.../ but instead /dʂt͡ʂ.../
Affricates count as one phoneme, not their constituent plosive and fricative.
E.g., Dszczaszkł /dʂt͡ʂaʂkw/


Syllable initial /j/ will not not trigger palatalization in following consonants, if in a cluster, but will raise the nucleus slightly; if the nucleus is /i/ it becomes, already is, or can be assumed to be, lengthened.



  1. Word initial /p/ written as |b| (e.g. bogai /ˈpo.gaj/), same for the other bilabial consonant pair, /f/ as |v| (e.g. vłe /fwe/) /
  2. |j| When on the boundary between, or in a stressed syllable (e.g. (again) bogai /ˈpo.gaj/ & jéve /ˈji:.ve/), |i| otherwise.
  3. Interchangable, the forms without diacritics are prefered informally and in handwriting, whilst in typing and formal writing the forms with diacritics are prefered. For /w/, |w| is less formal, but can be used interchangeably.
